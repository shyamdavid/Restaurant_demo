Our Story

Cooked with Love,
Served with Pride

Bangin was born from a simple dream — to bring the most authentic flavours of Chennai to every table. What started as a small roadside stall has grown into a beloved neighbourhood institution, loved by locals and visitors alike.

Every recipe is prepared fresh each day using locally sourced ingredients, traditional spices, and techniques passed down through generations. No shortcuts. No compromises.
